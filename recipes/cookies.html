Chocolate chip Cookies

Description

This is the most delicious recipe for chocolate chip cookies you have ever tried. Follow along this and you should have a chocolate delight in hands!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of baking soda
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. On a bowl, mix the sugars, the salt and the melted butter until there's a uniform mix
  2. Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the mixture and mix
  3. Sift the flour and baking soda on top of the mixture and mix
  4. Now, mix in the chocolate chips slowly to not break them apart
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  6. On a tray, put a sheet of parchment paper, and form little balls of the prepared dough
  7. Put your tray into the oven between 12 to 15 minutes, at 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Let the finished cookies rest for 5 minutes, then enjoy!
